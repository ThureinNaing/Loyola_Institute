Director's message!

Welcome to LIBA. LIBA is a Jesuit Business school. It is driven by Ignatian values of Magis (Excellence), Cura Personalis (Care of the individual), and Ad Maiorem Dei Gloriam (For the Greater Glory of God). We form leaders who excel in the fields they choose to lead. That excellence is guided by values and ethics. The ways we teach and the ways our students learn, we make every effort, are relevant, innovative and dynamic.
